Race Course

DETAIL ABOUT RACE COURSE

Running through the city of Monrovia, Liberia’s blooming capital, will be one of the most exciting race experiences
of your life. The Liberia Marathon 2022 will take you through the most scenic areas the city has to offer. Running on the
major roads, you will pass the historic downtown, lively and colourful residential areas and the emerging commercial
district. In the longer race categories, a stretch of Monrovia’s unique city beaches is a much part of the race as the
triumphal appearance on the iconic Tubman Boulevard before the finish line at the freshly renovated SKD Sportscomplex comes into sight.



COURSE OUTLINE FOR THE LIBERIA MARATHON 2022 EVENT

All race will take place on the same round course with different starting areas, but the same finish line.
Here are the details:

Full marathon (42.2k/ 26.2miles): Race start is at the SKD Sportscomplex, the national stadium located, and the course
will take the runners “all around the the world”, orbitting Paynesville and Monrovia in a large circle before coming back
to the SKD Sportscomplex. While the first half of the cours is fairly flat, right after passing the half-marathon mark a few
steep surprises will be awaiting our longest race contestants.

The Liberia Marathon event consists

includes a full marathon, a 10K foot
